00024 /// @section intro Introduction
00025 ///
00026 /// The Facility type plays a primary role in Cyclus.  A Facility
00027 /// facility is where offers and requests are generated and transmitted to a
00028 /// ResourceExchange and where shipments of material, issued by the exchange, are
00029 /// executed. The algorithms to determine what offers and requests are issued and
00030 /// how material shipments are handled are the primary differentiators between
00031 /// different Facility implementations.
00032 ///
00033 /// Like all agent implementations, there are a number of implementations that
00034 /// are distributed as part of the core Cyclus application as well as
00035 /// implementations contributed by third-party developers.  The links below
00036 /// describe additional parameters necessary for the complete definition of a
00037 /// facility of that implementation.
00038 ///
00039 /// @section available Available Core Implementations
00040 ///
00041 /// - SourceFacility: A facility that can produce a constant (or
00042 /// infinite) amount of some commodity
00043 /// - SinkFacility:  A facility that can consume a constant (or infinite)
00044 /// amount of some commodity
00045 /// - NullFacility: A facility that consumes a constant amount of one
00046 /// commodity and produces a constant amount of another
00047 ///
00048 ///
00049 /// @section anticipated Anticipated Core Implementations
00050 ///
00051 /// Developers are encouraged to add to this list and create pages that
00052 /// describe the detailed behavior of these agents.
00053 ///
00054 /// - RecipeReactor: A facility that consumes a fixed fresh fuel recipe
00055 /// one a time scale governed by reactor cycle lengths and batch sizes,
00056 /// and produced a fixed/corresponding spent fuel recipe at the same
00057 /// frequency - SeparationsMatrixFacility: A facility that consumes a
00058 /// fixed quantity of material of one commodity and produces many
00059 /// different output streams with the input nuclides distributed across
00060 /// those output streams according to a fixed matrix
00061 /// - EnrichmentFacility: A facility that offers a fixed quantity of SWUs
00062 /// to accomplish enrichment of material
00063 ///
00064 /// @section thirdparty Third-party Implementations
